About The Project, Me, Us

Methodology

Data for UK Salaries Analyser is meticulously collected from the GOV.UK job listing website, employing web scraping techniques facilitated by the BeautifulSoup and Requests libraries.

Utilizing Jupyter Notebooks, my data processing pipeline involves thorough exploration and cleaning:

  1. Correction of data types.
  2. Extraction of salaries from text.
  3. Removal of outliers.
  4. Imputation of null values.

To ensure the accuracy and reliability of my results, I conducted a comparative analysis against talent.com, validating my findings and affirming their integrity.

UK Salaries Analyser is a prototype poised for future enhancements. Anticipate the inclusion of international job data beyond the UK, as well as additional tools such as a tax calculator and skills analysis features.
